Community

The openSUSE Project since 2005 is a worldwide community of people dedicated to making open source software, documentation and artwork as well as advocating for open source. If this is your first introduction to what we do, go ahead and check out what we have in store.

Learn more

Events

Every couple of months some members of the community meet up for various convents, meetups and booths. We even have a yearly conference! We love being able to talk, share and discuss openSUSE eye to eye, and we can guarantee that you will love it too.

Check them out!

Contribute

Not just software, but documentation, translation, artwork, advocacy and other teams need your help. If you have a particular set of skills, we guarantee you will find something to do.
